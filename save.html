Savings Account

Saving is one of the best things you can do with your money. When you save, you put money away, and over time, it grows just by staying in a savings account. Saving helps you prepare for the big things you want in the future. If you're young, you don't need to worry about opening a savings account right now. When it's time, a parent or guardian will help you. For now, just remember that saving is important, so you're ready when it's time to start!

Snow Savings Account

In Boundless Math, you can save your Snow just like putting money in an actual savings account. When you save your Snow, you can't spend it on packs of cards right away, but you'll get extra Snow while it's saved. This extra Snow will help you buy even more packs later. You can save as much Snow as you want and take it out whenever you want. Plus, your saved Snow will grow by 1% for every question you get right. And don't worry, it won't lose any value if you get a question wrong. 1% might seem small, but it adds up quickly, and you'll be surprised at how much you save!
